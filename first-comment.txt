👋 Welcome to CakeMania.org! 

🎮 Quick Start Guide:
1. The game takes about 1 minute to load - please be patient
2. Works best with Google Chrome browser
3. If the game doesn't start, try refreshing the page

💡 Pro Tips for New Players:
• Start with the tutorial to learn basic controls
• Prioritize upgrading your ovens first
• Keep an eye on customer patience meters
• Use the TV to keep waiting customers happy
• Remember to save your progress regularly

🎯 Game Goals:
• Serve customers quickly
• Earn money to upgrade equipment
• Build your bakery empire
• Complete all 48 exciting levels
